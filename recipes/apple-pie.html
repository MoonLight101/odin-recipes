Apple Pie


Image from freepik

Description

Classic "Sharlotka". An important dessert of Soviet and post-Soviet history.
Light, fluffy dough, maximum apple filling - charlotte has always had the image of a pleasant, simple and at the same time delicious and dietary dish.
We strongly recommend taking sour apples. They can be put both raw and lightly caramelized in a frying pan. And it is better not to spare sugar.
It is magically distributed in the right amount in the dough, and the excess form a sweet crust.

Ingredients

Servings: 12

  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 5 chicken eggs
  • 7 apples
  • 1 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • ½ teaspoon of leavening agent
  • 1 cup of wheat flour

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven. Separate the whites from the yolks. Beat the whites into a stiff foam with a pinch of salt, gradually adding sugar.
  2. Continue beating, adding yolks one at a time, then baking powder and flour. The dough should have the consistency of sour cream.
  3. Grease a baking tray with vegetable oil. Pour half of the dough onto the baking tray, spread the apples evenly into slices, and pour the other half of the dough over it.
  4. Place the baking tray in a preheated oven. Keep it at 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) for 3 minutes, then reduce to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C) and bake for 30-40 minutes.
